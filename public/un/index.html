Peace and Security

The UN maintains international peace and security, gives humanitarian assistance to those in need, protects human rights, and upholds international law.

Sustainable Development

The 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development, adopted by all United Nations Member States in 2015, provides a shared blueprint for peace and prosperity for people and the planet, now and into the future.

Human Rights

The UN works to promote and protect human rights for all, advocating for the protection of human rights and assisting States in carrying out their responsibilities.
